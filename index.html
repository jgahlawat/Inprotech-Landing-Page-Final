Inprotech® could be your perfect
IP Management Solution

Meeting client expectations doesn’t give your firm an advantage — exceeding them does. Inprotech delivers your firm that advantage to the best.



IP Matter Management

Inprotech IP Matter Management provides the essentials — case and name management, workflow automation, records management, data verification, case import, e‑filing, electronic data exchange, document and form generation, custom content, file tracking, word processing integration, and foreign language support. Inprotech enhances a firm’s productivity through automation of complex or routine tasks.


Time Recording and Billing

Inprotech is used to capture, cost, and analyse time based activities and recoverable expenses. It has been specifically designed to accommodate foreign currency transactions, agent fees, and disbursements. Timesheet can be adapted to a range of work practices. Inprotech Billing allows a firm to perform complex invoicing tasks such as electronic billing (LEDES format), foreign currency conversion, debtor to creditor offset transactions, and recovery of expense charges.


Client Access and Marketing

With Inprotech, intellectual property case management information is available to the firm's clients anytime, from anywhere. Client Access offers tailored views for different roles and lets clients securely access their portfolio of cases, run reports, and interact with their case managers. Inprotech Marketing goes beyond analysing current clients to managing the entire lead cycle — including coordinating marketing campaigns, generating leads, and sending email invitations. Marketing can also be used to monitor direct marketing activities and keep a record of incoming and outgoing phone calls, letters and email.

Why Inprotech?

People, tools and technologies to support the entire IP lifecycle.

As a leading IPMS, Inprotech helps lower costs and manage risk. It brings value by providing insight for decision makers and automating routine tasks. Automatic calculation of deadlines in Inprotech, coupled with laws being maintained through the CPA Global Law Update Service, underpins the firm’s risk management strategy.

With Inprotech there’s no need for staff to be switching between systems throughout the day. By using Inprotech as your primary work portal, content from other systems and websites can be accessed from within Inprotech. Try it with your accounting systems, IP Office searches, Agent websites.

Flexibility is the foundation of Inprotech. This ensures that each firm can have a system entirely suited to the way it works, even to the extent of integrating content from virtually any other internal or external system into the Inprotech Portal.

Case data comparison with national Patent and Trademark offices (e.g. USPTO TSDR database) provides the option for automated docketing where data and documents downloaded according to your schedule are compared with corresponding information in Inprotech, allowing for automatic updates in Inprotech and the firm’s integrated document management system.
